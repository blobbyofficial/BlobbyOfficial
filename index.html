    Process

    How the edit gets from raw footage to final export.

    The workflow stays simple on purpose: shape the hook, control the pace, and finish the delivery cleanly so the result feels premium instead of overworked.

    01

    Hook the first second

    Start with the strongest beat, the clearest visual, or the fastest payoff so the cut earns attention immediately.

    02

    Shape the pacing

    Trim dead air, place motion where it matters, and keep the edit readable so the video feels sharp instead of chaotic.

    03

    Deliver clean versions

    Finish with polished exports, platform-ready crops, and the preset support needed to keep future edits consistent.
